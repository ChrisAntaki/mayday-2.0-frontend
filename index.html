Mayday Phase II Begins Now

In 2014, we rallied more than 50,000 Americans to support a fight for fundamental reform in the way campaigns are funded – to reduce the influence of money in politics, by increasing the influence of people in politics.

But in the campaigns, we didn’t move the ball far enough.

So in 2015, we’re doing something different.

Help us find allies in Congress

We want to close the gap on a majority in Congress – both Republicans and Democrats – committed to fundamental reform.

We need you to help us identify who our allies should be.

Click here to tell us who you think we could persuade, and why.

We don’t just want the obvious. We want to identify members of Congress who could be leaders, and why. We’re not interested in dirt, or anything negative. We’re interested in who you believe could step up to be real leaders.

The citizens’ lobby

Then on May 1 – May Day – we will launch a major new platform to support the biggest citizen lobbying campaign that we possibly can – to recruit a majority of leaders to get us our democracy back. Sign up here to be reminded and recruited.

We can’t do this without you

Our plan’s success relies on the support of our community. We are counting on you to be part of the movement to restore the principles our republic was founded on and end the systemic corruption in Washington, D.C.
